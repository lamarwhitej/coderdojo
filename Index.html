Meet Our Team

Coder Dojo was founded in Dublin, Ireland. It is a company whose goal is to get children to code internationally. Coder Dojo collabrative was created by two moms: Nikole Vaughn, and Malene Lawrence. They wanted the children of San Antonio to learn how to code as well as children around the world. The children who attend Coder Dojo collaborative are called Ninjas! Ninjas earn belts for completing projects, similar to a karate ninja. Donate Here!
